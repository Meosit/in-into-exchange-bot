__National Bank of Belarus__
- Site: http://www.nbrb.by/engl
- Names: `NBRB`, `НБРБ`
- Base currency: `BYN`
- Supported currencies: `BYN`, `USD`, `EUR`, `RUB`, `UAH`, `PLN`, `CZK`, `GBP`, `JPY`, `CNY`, `KZT`, `CHF`, `BGN`, `TRY`, `CAD`, `ISK`, `DKK`, `SEK`, `NOK`
